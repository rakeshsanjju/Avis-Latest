About Dr. Rajah V Koppala

Dr. Rajah V Koppala is a highly experienced Interventional Radiologist and Vascular Specialist with over 15 years of dedicated practice in minimally invasive vascular procedures. He completed his medical degree from prestigious institutions and pursued advanced fellowship training in interventional radiology. Currently serving as the Director of Interventional Radiology at leading medical centers, Dr. Koppala has performed thousands of successful procedures, consistently achieving excellent patient outcomes with minimal complications.
